Skinny Mann

Just a terrible 2D platformer, or is it...?

Download the lates version of the game HERE

About:
Skinny Mann is a platformer game made by jSdCool that may or may not contain multiple dimentions.
Run, Jump, and play your way through a series of pre built levels that may or may not contain a story. You can also create your own levels or play ones made by friends.
Have frieds? Play againsed or with them in multyplayer.
